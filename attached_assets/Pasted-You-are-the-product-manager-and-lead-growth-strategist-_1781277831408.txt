You are the product manager and lead growth strategist for Pepcheck.

The current website is an MVP for comparing compounded GLP-1 providers. Do not redesign everything from scratch. Instead, improve the existing product with a focus on validating demand and generating traffic.

Context:

Pepcheck helps users compare GLP-1 providers by:

* Monthly price
* Medication type (tirzepatide, semaglutide, etc.)
* State availability
* Provider legitimacy and compliance status
* Reviews and ratings

Our goal is NOT to immediately sell featured listings. Our first objective is to become the trusted comparison platform people use before choosing a provider.

Priority roadmap:

PHASE 1: TRAFFIC & VALIDATION

1. Create SEO-focused landing pages:

   * Cheapest Tirzepatide Providers
   * Cheapest Semaglutide Providers
   * Best GLP-1 Providers by State
   * Compare GLP-1 Providers
   * GLP-1 Price Tracker

2. Add state pages:

   * /texas
   * /florida
   * /california
   * etc.

Each page should dynamically show providers available in that state.

3. Add a searchable comparison table:

   * Filter by state
   * Filter by medication
   * Sort by lowest price
   * Sort by highest rated
   * Sort by fastest delivery

4. Add clear CTAs:

   * Compare Providers
   * Find Providers in Your State
   * View Cheapest Options

5. Add analytics tracking for:

   * Search usage
   * Filter usage
   * Provider clicks
   * Most viewed states

PHASE 2: DATA & TRUST

6. Create a provider database structure with:

   * Provider name
   * Website
   * Medication types
   * Monthly price
   * Consultation fee
   * State availability
   * Review score
   * Last verified date
   * Compliance notes

7. Add trust signals:

   * "Last Updated" timestamps
   * Methodology page
   * Disclaimer page
   * About page
   * Contact page

8. Add provider profile pages with:

   * Pricing breakdown
   * Pros and cons
   * State availability
   * Review summary
   * Compliance information

PHASE 3: FUTURE MONETIZATION

9. Build infrastructure for referral tracking:

   * Outbound click tracking
   * Unique provider referral links
   * Lead attribution

10. Add placeholder areas for future sponsored placements, but do not make them prominent yet.

Design goals:

* Professional healthcare comparison platform
* Similar trust level to NerdWallet, G2, or Compare the Market
* Clean, data-first design
* Mobile-first responsive experience
* Fast loading pages
* Strong SEO structure

Important:
Focus first on building traffic-generating pages, comparison functionality, trust signals, and provider data architecture. Do not spend time building subscription systems, payment systems, or complex user accounts until traffic validation exists.
